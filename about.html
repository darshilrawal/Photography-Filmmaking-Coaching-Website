Our Story

Where Passion Meets Profession

Founded in 2015, FrameCraft Academy emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between creative passion and professional excellence. What started as a small photography workshop in Mumbai has grown into one of India's premier institutions for visual arts education.

Our founders, themselves seasoned professionals in photography and filmmaking, recognized the need for practical, industry-relevant education that traditional institutions often lacked. They envisioned a space where aspiring artists could learn not just the technical aspects of their craft, but also develop the creative vision and business acumen needed to thrive in the competitive world of visual media.

Today, FrameCraft Academy stands as a testament to that vision, having trained over 2,500 students who now work across the globe in diverse rolesâ€”from wedding photographers and documentary filmmakers to Hollywood colorists and advertising directors.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide transformative education that empowers aspiring photographers and filmmakers with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to create compelling visual stories. We are committed to offering hands-on training with industry-standard equipment, mentorship from working professionals, and a supportive community that nurtures creativity and innovation.

We believe that everyone with a passion for visual storytelling deserves access to quality education that can turn their dreams into reality.

Our Vision

To be the leading academy for visual arts education in Asia, known for producing industry-ready professionals who push the boundaries of creative storytelling. We envision a future where our alumni are at the forefront of visual innovation, setting new standards in photography, filmmaking, and digital content creation.

We aspire to create a global network of visual artists who inspire, educate, and elevate the craft for generations to come.
